## 关于我们 | About Us

酉术量子（UnitaryLab）由上海交通大学顶尖科研团队于2025年1月创立，是一家专注于**量子算法原始创新与产业落地**的科技企业。我们致力于构建通用的卓越量子计算应用能力与基础设施，推动“超算-智算-量算”三算融合，成为具备全球竞争力的量子算法创新赋能者。

UnitaryLab is a quantum technology company founded in January 2025 by top research teams from Shanghai Jiao Tong University. We focus on original quantum algorithm innovation and industrial deployment, building universal quantum computing capabilities and infrastructure to advance the integration of supercomputing, intelligent computing, and quantum computing. Our mission is to become a globally competitive enabler of quantum algorithm innovation for science and engineering applications.

## 核心产品：UnitaryLab 量子科学计算平台
### Core Product: UnitaryLab Quantum Scientific Computing Platform

UnitaryLab 是**智能体驱动的全栈量子科学计算软件平台**，为科研、开发与行业应用提供一站式解决方案：
- 🧠 **智能体增强引擎**：内置量子科学计算Agent，实现从问题定义到算法生成的全链路赋能
- ⚡ **高性能量子模拟器**：支持高效量子线路设计与模拟
- 📚 **专业量子算法库**：覆盖线性代数、偏微分方程求解、量子优化、量子机器学习等核心场景
- 🛠️ **软硬一体化方案**：适配专用模拟量子计算机，提供可小型化、定制化的垂直场景解决方案

UnitaryLab is an **Agent-driven full-stack quantum scientific computing platform**, providing one-stop solutions for research, development, and industrial applications:
- 🧠 **Agent-Powered Engine**: Built-in Quantum Scientific Computing Agent enables end-to-end empowerment from problem definition to algorithm generation
- ⚡ **High-Performance Quantum Simulator**: Supports efficient quantum circuit design and simulation
- 📚 **Professional Quantum Algorithm Library**: Covers linear algebra, PDE solving, quantum optimization, and quantum machine learning
- 🛠️ **Hardware-Software Integrated Solutions**: Adaptable to dedicated analog quantum computers, providing customized solutions for vertical scenarios

- 🎯 **强大研发团队**：专注科学与工程领域量子算法创新的团队，拥有超15人的核心算法研发团队
- 📈 **指数级计算加速**：针对金融、材料、气象等场景实现多项式/指数级加速，大幅降低算力需求
- 🏆 **权威学术认可**：算法成果入选国家级与校级奖项，获得国内外学术界广泛认可

- 🔬 **Internationally Pioneering Algorithms**: First to propose the Schrödingerisation algorithm, overcoming the industry bottleneck of traditional quantum algorithms being unable to directly solve scientific computing problems
- 🎯 **Strong R&D Team**: A team focused on quantum algorithm innovation for science and engineering, with a core algorithm R&D team of over 15 members
- 📈 **Exponential Computational Acceleration**: Achieves polynomial/exponential acceleration for scenarios in finance, materials, and weather, significantly reducing computational requirements
- 🏆 **Authoritative Academic Recognition**: Algorithm achievements have been selected for national and university-level awards, gaining widespread recognition in the academic community both domestically and internationally
